About Project

Agri-Nigrani System

Smart Farming, Secure Crops, and AI-Driven Guidance for Every Farmer.

Our Mission

"To empower farmers by reducing resource waste, protecting crops 24/7, and bringing expert AI guidance in their native language—making advanced agriculture accessible and highly profitable."

Key Features for Farmers

psychology
Expert AI in native Bengali

Instantly diagnose plant diseases using Gemini 2.5 AI. Get actionable remedies in Bengali to protect your yield.

water_pump
Smart Water & Pump Safety

Automated irrigation with built-in hardware Dry Run Protection—saving your expensive pump from burning out if water stops flowing.

water_drop
Fertilizer Runoff Monitor

Tracks nutrient loss during rain or heavy irrigation so you don't waste expensive fertilizers.

security
False-Alarm-Free Security

Advanced dual-sensor fusion ensures you're only alerted for actual intruders, ignoring wind or small animals.
